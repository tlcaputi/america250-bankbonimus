The former governor's mansion and one-time home of Declaration signer Richard Stockton,
Morven shows its permanent "Historic Morven: A Window into America's Past" plus special
exhibitions "Northern Family, Southern Ties" and "Morven Revealed," surrounded by
restored historic gardens. Museum open Wed–Sun 10am–4pm; the gardens are free and open
daily dawn to dusk.
