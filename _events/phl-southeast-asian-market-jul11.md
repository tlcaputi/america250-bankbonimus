Philadelphia's beloved open-air Southeast Asian Market returns to FDR Park every Saturday
and Sunday, with 70+ vendors selling Cambodian, Thai, Vietnamese, Lao and other street
food and goods. For July the market sits mid-park near Edgewood Lake. Free to browse.
