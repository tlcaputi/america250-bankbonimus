A North-American-premiere blockbuster exhibit exploring the storytelling, technology and
imagination behind Universal's theme parks — 100+ artifacts and 20 interactive activities
across eight galleries, from coaster hardware to costumes and storyboards. Timed tickets
($41–$47) include general museum admission.
