The Doylestown Farmers Market runs every Saturday from 8am to 1pm, April 18 through
November 21, 2026 (managed by the Bucks County Foodshed Alliance). Local produce,
baked goods, meats, cheeses, flowers, and prepared foods in the heart of Doylestown
Borough. Free to attend.
