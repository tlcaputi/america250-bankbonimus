Hunterdon County's marquee America 250 event: the Hunterdon Symphony performs patriotic,
made-in-America works (Sousa, Copland, Bernstein, Horner) as part of the county's free
Music Under the Stars series, opened by a reading of the Declaration of Independence and
capped by what organizers bill as "the biggest fireworks display the county has ever
seen." Bring blankets and lawn chairs. Rain date is Thursday July 9; in lighter weather
the concert (no fireworks) moves indoors to Hunterdon Central High School, 84 Route 31,
doors 6pm, no food/drink in the auditorium. Call (908) 782-7275 after 4:30pm for the
weather call.
