This exhibition at the newly reopened Princeton University Art Museum investigates mid-century photographic modernism through roughly 150 photographs by Minor White, Aaron Siskind, and Harry Callahan, along with correspondence and other materials exploring how photography was taught and viewed. Museum admission is free to all.
