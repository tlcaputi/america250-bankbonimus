The Trenton City Museum's flagship juried show, Ellarslie Open 43, fills the Italianate
Ellarslie Mansion in Cadwalader Park with 127 works by 125 artists across many genres,
on view through September 6. Free to visit; open Thursday–Saturday 12–4pm and Sunday
1–4pm.
