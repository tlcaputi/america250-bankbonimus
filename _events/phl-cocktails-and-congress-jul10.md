Historic Philadelphia's immersive theater experience transports guests to 1774 to witness the debates
of the First Continental Congress in the very hall where it met. Watch the Founding Fathers argue over
independence — including the heated exchange between John Adams and John Dickinson — alongside the
perspectives of enslaved and free Black communities. Ticket ($50) includes two cocktails or mocktails.
