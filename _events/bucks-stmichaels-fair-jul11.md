The 72nd annual St. Michael's Fair runs nightly through Saturday, July 12, 2026,
on the grounds of St. Michael the Archangel Catholic Church in Levittown. Admission
is free; the fair offers amusement rides, carnival games, and classic fair food.
Ride wristbands and tickets are sold online, at community retailers, and on-site.
This entry covers the fair's continuation into the July 6–12 window (the original
July 4 date is already in the guide).
