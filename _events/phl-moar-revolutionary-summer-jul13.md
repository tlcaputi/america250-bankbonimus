The Museum of the American Revolution runs daily "Revolutionary Summer" programming — living-history interpreters, hands-on activities in Revolution Place, and family programs in the Patriots Gallery — through the summer. A fitting Old City stop during the 250th, and open on the otherwise-quiet Monday of All-Star Week.
