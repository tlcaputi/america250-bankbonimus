The L.E.A.D. Fest State Fair returns to Mercer County Park with a carnival midway, rides, live music, a circus, food and agriculture displays, with proceeds supporting Law Enforcement Against Drugs & Violence prevention programs. The July 4 evening features a patriotic drone show, and the fair closes with fireworks on July 5. Ten days of family entertainment tied to America's 250th summer.
