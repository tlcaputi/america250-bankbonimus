The capstone day of Trenton Heritage Week. At noon, Trenton Kiwanis and the City stage
the 250th Annual Proclamation of the Declaration of Independence at the 23 S. Warren St.
plaza, followed by History Fest250 (exhibits, demonstrations and family activities
across downtown) at 1pm, a two-mile Historic Downtown Trenton Walking Tour at 3pm, an
International Proclamation of the Declaration at 6pm, and a free Blawenburg Band colonial
concert at 6:30pm. A full day of free Revolutionary-history programming in the capital.
