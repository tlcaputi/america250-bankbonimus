King of Prussia Town Center's free "Live Music on the Green" runs every Thursday
from April through October, 6–8 PM, on the green space in front of the Town
Center's restaurants. Bring a chair or blanket and enjoy local bands steps from
the shops and dining. July 9 falls within the series.
