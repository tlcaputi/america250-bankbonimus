As part of the nation's 250th, Grounds For Sculpture — the 42-acre contemporary sculpture
park in Hamilton — is open until 9pm Friday–Sunday and drops admission to just $5 per
person from 5–9pm on summer Fridays through September 4 (free for members). A relaxed
golden-hour wander among hundreds of monumental sculptures. A "Beading Circle Basics"
workshop (6–7:30pm, ticketed) also runs this evening.
