Middlesex County's free Music-in-the-Park summer series holds Wednesday concerts at
Thompson Park in Monroe (2–3:30pm) and Raritan Bay Waterfront Park in South Amboy
(6:30–8:30pm), running July through early September. Bring a blanket or lawn chair;
the individual performers for this date are not listed on the county schedule.
