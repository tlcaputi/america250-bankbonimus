The Sellersville Fire Department's 2026 carnival returns July 7–11 with five nights
of rides, games, carnival food, and nightly bingo at 7pm. A fireworks display caps
Friday, July 10. Wristband nights run Tuesday through Thursday from 6pm to close
(presale wristbands $30, $40 at the gate). Free parking and an on-site ATM. Admission
to the grounds is free.
