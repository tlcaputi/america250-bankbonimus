A gravity-powered soap box car race down Fayette Street, a Conshohocken Independence Day tradition running since 1951 — making 2026 its 75th year. Kids race homemade cars from 8th Avenue to 4th Avenue, with timed heats and a chance to advance to national competition. A longstanding community spectacle held every Fourth of July.
