Full-dome planetarium shows at the NJ State Museum, one of the largest planetariums in the country. July 2026 titles include "The Great Solar System Adventure," "Messengers of Time and Space," and "One Sky Project." Summer weekday shows run at 11:30 a.m., 1:30 p.m. and 2:30 p.m.; tickets $10 adults, $5 children 3-12. Museum galleries are free.
