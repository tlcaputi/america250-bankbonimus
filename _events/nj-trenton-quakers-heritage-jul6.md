Part of Trenton Heritage Week's America 250 programming. Tour the historic Quaker
meetinghouse and burial ground — where Declaration signer George Clymer is buried —
with reminiscences and history talks. Historian Frederick Millner presents "Quakers!
Choose a Side" on the Society of Friends in Revolutionary New Jersey (9:30am & 2pm),
and attorney-historian Marc A. McKithen leads an eight-block guided walking tour of
colonial Trenton at 11am. Repeats Tuesday July 7.
