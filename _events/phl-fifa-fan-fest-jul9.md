Philadelphia's official FIFA World Cup 26 Fan Festival on Lemon Hill runs daily through
the tournament final on Jul 19 — giant screens showing every match, food, live music and
cultural programming. Match-day access is free but requires advance registration; some
non-match days feature ticketed concerts and community events.
