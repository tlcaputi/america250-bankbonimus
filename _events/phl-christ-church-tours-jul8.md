The 1744 "Nation's Church" where George Washington, Benjamin Franklin, Betsy Ross, and other founders
worshipped. Open to visitors daily 11am–5pm with 20–30 minute tours: guided ($10 adults / $5 youth),
self-guided ($5 / $2), or combo tickets with the Christ Church Burial Ground ($8 / $3). A cornerstone stop
in the historic district for the semiquincentennial year.
