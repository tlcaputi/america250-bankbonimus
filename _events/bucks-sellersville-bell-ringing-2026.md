Sellersville Borough joins the national 'Let Freedom Ring' bell ringing, sounding bells once for each of the 13 original colonies to commemorate the signing of the Declaration of Independence. For America's 250th the ceremony includes a historical reenactment and showcases the newly renovated Sellersville Museum (reopened late 2025).
