A Fourth of July running tradition on its 44th year, with a 1-mile Fun Run, certified 5K, and certified 10K starting from the main field at Washington Crossing Historic Park. Runners follow in the footsteps of Washington's troops along the Delaware. Proceeds benefit the Upper Makefield Emergency Fund, UMBA scholarships, the local fire department, and the historic park. Race-day registration is $30 (1-mile) and $50 (5K/10K).
