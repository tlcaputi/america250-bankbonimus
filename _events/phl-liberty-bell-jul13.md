See the cracked Liberty Bell up close in its glass pavilion, with free exhibits tracing its history from the Pennsylvania State House to its role as a symbol of liberty and abolition. Free and self-guided, open daily 9 a.m.–5 p.m. An easy anchor for a historic-district Monday.
