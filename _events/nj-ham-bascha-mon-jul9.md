A retrospective of 93-year-old New Jersey artist Bascha Mon, pairing her 1970s paintings with recent works on paper. Curator Mary Birmingham presents these "metaphorical landscapes of the imagination" sourced from the artist's unconscious mind. Shown alongside other summer exhibitions including the 2026 Claybash ceramics show and Christine Romanell's augmented-reality installation.
