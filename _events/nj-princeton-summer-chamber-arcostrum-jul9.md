The 59th season of the free Princeton University Summer Chamber Concerts continues with ArcoStrum, a violin-and-guitar duo blending Eastern and Western traditions with Chinese and classical instrumentation. The program spans Piazzolla, Vivaldi, Scarlatti, John Williams, Gnattali, and contemporary works. All concerts are free and open to the public in Richardson Auditorium.
