'Step into Revolutionary America': military encampment, craft demos, musket and artillery firing, children's activities, and Declaration readings (4–8pm), then a fife-and-drum performance, concert, food trucks and fireworks over the Delaware (8–9:30pm). Bowman's Hill Tower access included.
