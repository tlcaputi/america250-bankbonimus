Middlesex County's Plays-in-the-Park stages the Tony-winning musical "1776," which dramatizes John Adams, Benjamin Franklin, and Thomas Jefferson pressing the Second Continental Congress to declare independence. A fitting America's-250th selection under the stars at the outdoor Capestro Theatre. $8 adults, $5 seniors 60+, free for children 12 and under.
