Meet Dr. Franklin in the courtyard where he once lived, beneath a mulberry tree. Visitors can ask
questions and hear Franklin (or his companions) hold forth on his life as a printer, inventor, statesman,
and Philadelphian. A free program created with Independence National Historical Park and the NPS; runs
Fridays and Saturdays at 1:00 and 2:30pm (so also Saturday, July 11).
