Elfreth's Alley bills itself as the nation's oldest continuously inhabited residential street. The 40-minute
guided history tour walks the full length of the alley with an expert guide and includes entry to the two
museum houses (Sat & Sun at 2:30pm; $10 adults / $5 kids 7-12). The museum itself is open Fri–Sun 12–4pm
($3 adults / $2 kids) for self-guided visits.
