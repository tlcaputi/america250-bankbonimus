An after-hours, exclusive-access evening inside Independence Hall. A costumed Colonial host guides guests
through the Pennsylvania State House, encountering the Founding Fathers in scenes that reveal the urgency,
disagreement, and uncertainty of 1776. The night ends with celebratory toasts at a nearby tavern. Ticket
($145) includes a cocktail or mocktail; meet at the Independence Visitor Center information desk.
