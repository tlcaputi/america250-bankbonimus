The Abington Art Center's free summer concert series runs Thursday evenings June 18–
July 16, and continues July 9 on the grounds of Alverthorpe Manor. Grounds open at
5:30pm with an opening act at 6:30pm and headliner at 7:15pm. All-ages, with regional
bands, food trucks, and refreshments. Rain or shine (canceled only for hazardous
weather). Specific July 9 act to be confirmed.
