One of upper Bucks County's signature summer traditions: a juried outdoor arts festival on the grounds of riverside Tinicum Park, with 100+ artists and craftspeople, live music on stage, food, and children's activities. A benefit for the Tinicum Civic Association. Wheelchair accessible; free parking. Runs both Saturday and Sunday.
