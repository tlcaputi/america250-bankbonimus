A celebration marking the newly reopened First Bank of the United States, launching a '52 Weeks of Firsts' program with guest speakers, a sculpture unveiling and historic deep dives into Philadelphia's pioneering achievements. The 1797 First Bank building reopened to the public as part of the Semiquincentennial.
