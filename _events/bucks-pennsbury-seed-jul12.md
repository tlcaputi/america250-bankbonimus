Living History Theater at Pennsbury Manor, the reconstructed 17th-century country
estate of Pennsylvania founder William Penn, Sunday, July 12 from 1–4pm. The "Seed of
a Nation" program uses costumed interpreters and the site's exhibits to explore Penn's
vision and colonial-era Pennsylvania. Admission applies.
