The museum's marquee America 250 special exhibition (presented by Griffin Catalyst) traces
the 250-year global history and legacy of the Declaration of Independence, gathering rare
documents, art and artifacts from around the world for the first time. Accessible features
include a Braille tactile book and ASL film translations.
