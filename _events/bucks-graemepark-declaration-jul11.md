A 250th-anniversary celebration at historic Graeme Park featuring a brass trio
playing patriotic favorites and a reading of the Declaration of Independence by
costumed colonial reenactors. Includes period dance demonstrations, quill-pen
writing activities, and complimentary ice cream while supplies last.
