Milltown's long-running Independence Day celebration returns for America's 250th with a day of community events: a fishing derby, 5K race, the town parade, a duck race, hot-dog and pizza eating contests, a patriotic house contest, a bike contest and live music. Backed by New Jersey's RevolutionNJ semiquincentennial program.
