The Opus Outreach Foundation's free "Music on the Street" summer concert series
returns to downtown Newtown Borough every Thursday from July 9 through September 3.
Opening night features Anthony Wexler, a Philadelphia-based rock and acoustic
artist. Bring chairs and blankets; lemonade sales benefit the foundation.
