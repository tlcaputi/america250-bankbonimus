Frenchtown's signature summer street celebration of France's national holiday, held annually since 1997. The day includes an 11 AM-5 PM downtown scavenger hunt (collect 12 stamps at participating businesses for a chance to win a gift card), a Jazz Faction live performance and a Doug Miller magic show at TD Bank from noon to 3 PM, and an artisan vendor market of local makers throughout downtown.
