Franklin Township's annual Independence Day Celebration kicks off at 6 pm at the Municipal Complex with food trucks, local exhibitors, and children's activities. Musical entertainment comes from the Franklin Township Community Band and the New Jersey National Guard 63rd Army Band, with a fireworks display at about 9:15 pm.
