The free summer waterfront park at Penn's Landing returns with its signature hammock grove, LED-lit trees, floating gardens, boardwalk games, and barge bars and food. New for 2026 are Chiliboats and swan boats. A relaxed evening option on an otherwise quiet Monday of All-Star Week.
