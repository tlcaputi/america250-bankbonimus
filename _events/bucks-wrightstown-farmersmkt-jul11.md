The Wrightstown Farmers Market runs Saturdays 9am–noon at the Middletown Grange
Fairgrounds, May through Thanksgiving. Producer-only market with seasonal fruits and
vegetables, meats, eggs, baked goods, and prepared foods. Free to attend.
