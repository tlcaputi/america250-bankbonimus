Freshly reopened for the 250th (the refurbished West Wing came back online June 30, 2026), the **Great Essentials** exhibit is the one Independence Hall room you can walk into free and without a timed ticket. Inside are surviving original printings of the **Declaration of Independence** (a Dunlap broadside), the Articles of Confederation, and the U.S. Constitution — plus the silver **Syng inkstand** that, by tradition, was used to sign both the Declaration and the Constitution. The redesigned space adds interactive and augmented-reality displays. A perfect stop right before or after the July 8 Declaration reading a few steps away on Independence Square.
