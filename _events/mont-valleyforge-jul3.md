Day 1 of the NPS 50/250 weekend (Valley Forge's 50th as a park + America's 250th): Muhlenberg's Brigade ribbon-cutting and musket-firing demos, Camp Life with Spencer's Additional Regiment, surveying & mapmaking, two new exhibits ('Valley Forge Then and Now' and 'My Valley Forge Story'), and a storytelling fire-pit dedication.
