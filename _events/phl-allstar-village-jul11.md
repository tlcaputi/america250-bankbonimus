The interactive centerpiece of MLB All-Star Week takes over the Convention Center from
Jul 11 through Jul 14. Capital One All-Star Village packs in games, contests, video-gaming,
prizes, player meet-and-greets and ballpark food, plus the youth-focused Play Ball Park —
a baseball theme park with mini diamond, clinics, batting cages, pitching tunnels and a
kids' Home Run Derby.
