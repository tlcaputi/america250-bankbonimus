Buckingham Township's free summer concert series returns Thursday nights at the
Hansell Park Band Shell, 7–9 PM. The July 9 show features Phantom Trap. Bring
lawn chairs or a blanket; in case of bad weather the concert moves to the
Buckingham Springs Clubhouse.
