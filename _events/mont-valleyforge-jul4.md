The big day at Valley Forge: a giant Declaration of Independence signing, Courts Martial programs, live music by the Hot Taters at the National Memorial Arch, hut building, replica cannon and Conestoga wagon, artillery demos with the 13th Virginia Regiment, colonial-clothing try-on, and live Declaration readings at the Maurice Stephens House (10am, 12:30pm, 3pm).
