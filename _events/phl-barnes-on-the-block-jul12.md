The Barnes throws its free, family-friendly summer street festival on the Parkway, with DJs and live music, hands-on art activities, a craft market, food vendors, and a beer garden. Presented with PNC Arts Alive and Mural Arts Philadelphia; free gallery access to the Barnes collection and special exhibitions with registration.
