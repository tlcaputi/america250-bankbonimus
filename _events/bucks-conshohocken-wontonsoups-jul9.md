Opening night of the Borough of Conshohocken's free 2026 Summer Concert Series.
The Wonton Soups blend jazz, musical theater, marching band, funk, ska, swing,
pop and big band into a distinctive sound. Bring lawn chairs and blankets; the
first three shows of the season are held at Sutcliffe Park.
