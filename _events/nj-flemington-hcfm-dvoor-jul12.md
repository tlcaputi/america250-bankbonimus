The community farmers market at the historic Dvoor Farm, run in partnership with the Hunterdon Land Trust. Vendors bring seasonal produce, local meat, artisan cheeses, breads, native plants, cut flowers, honey, coffee, and ready-to-eat foods. Open every Sunday from early May through late November, 9 AM to 1 PM.
