A special exhibit at the Mercer Museum telling the story of the Bucks County
Loyalist Doan family and their associates, who plotted and stole in support of the
British cause during the American Revolution. On view through December 31, 2026;
included with general Mercer Museum admission. (Museum closed Mondays.)
