An invitational, multimedia group exhibition mounted for America's 250th at Bucks County Community College's Hicks Art Center Gallery in Newtown — artworks, artifacts and objects capturing evolutionary moments of the nation's 250-year history from human and animal perspectives, with significant historical loans alongside contemporary work. On view June 5 through July 25; free.
